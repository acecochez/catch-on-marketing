Ownership and Hosting Options

Default Hosting

Your project is hosted on Vercel with a format like your-website.vercel.app. Hosting access is not included unless you choose a paid hosting plan.

Full Control Option

If you want complete ownership, we can publish through GitHub Pages and transfer the repository directly to you. This gives you full access to the codebase and all future control over hosting.

How the Build Process Works

  • You agree to have a site created.
  • We confirm your specifications and produce a first iteration.
  • You request changes until the design matches your expectations.
  • Before payment, you will preview the site through video with a watermark.
  • Once you are satisfied and complete payment, you receive the live link along with full code ownership if requested.
